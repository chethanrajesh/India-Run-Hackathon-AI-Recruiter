Job Title: Senior MLOps Engineer
Location: Remote

Role Description:
We are looking for a Senior MLOps Engineer to lead the deployment, scaling, and monitoring of our machine learning models. You will bridge the gap between Data Science and Software Engineering, ensuring our models run efficiently in production.

Required Experience:
- 5+ years of experience in Software Engineering or DevOps.
- 2+ years of specific experience in MLOps or deploying machine learning models.

Required Skills:
- Python
- AWS (EC2, S3, SageMaker)
- Docker
- Kubernetes (EKS)
- CI/CD Pipelines (GitHub Actions or Jenkins)
- Terraform

Optional Skills:
- PyTorch / TensorFlow
- MLflow or Weights & Biases
- Kafka

Responsibilities:
- Containerize machine learning models using Docker.
- Deploy models to Kubernetes clusters on AWS.
- Build automated CI/CD pipelines for model training and deployment.
- Monitor model drift and infrastructure health.
- Collaborate with data scientists to optimize model latency and throughput.
